About Our Business

We are a family owned and operated ice cream truck business located in Chambersburg, Pennsylvania. In 2010, we purchased a retired DHL delivery truck and transformed it into the ice cream truck you can see today.

My wife, son, daughter and I started doing events in 2011. I originally built the truck to sell ice cream at car shows and cruise-ins, but since then our business has grown into serving at a variety of events, including: Car Shows, Sporting Events, Craft Shows, Corporate Events, Birthday Parties, Weddings, Church Events, Picnics, and any place an ice cream truck is needed!

Note: Due to the amount of prep work involved in operating our business, we require a "minimum in sales" to do private events. Please contact us for more information.
